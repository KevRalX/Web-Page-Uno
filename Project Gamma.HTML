What are we all about?
Well, we believe that the most dominant desire of mankind is self-expression. Not surprisingly, man has used different methods to satisfy this urge:
Drawing, Singing, Acting and yes, Poetry. Of course, these are only a few of the ways man has quenched this deep burning desire to be seen, heard and understood.
But why Poetry?
Well, unlike other forms of art, Poetry is alive, dynamic, and an absolute thriller. It has one major rule: THERE ARE NO RULES!
You have the choice to express yourself fully and freely without checking your grammar, without being afraid of being sanctioned. Poetry is a safe haven from the real world and yet it is the very breath of emotion, the cycle of motion and the spark of transformation
Our Slogan As Still Waters Is Simply The Embodiment Of What We Hope To Help You Experience As You Are Impacted By Our Content.
